About Our Team

Who are the headliners?

Lorem beauty was concieved by three students in their dorms. Sebastian Morris (left), James Bradley (top right) and Tina Ray (bottom left). 

They noticed that though genderless fashion soared, there was no inclusivess for genderless makeup. 

In 2019, they raise $1M in seedfund money and started Lorem Beauty as a way to lessen the gap in genderless makeup.
